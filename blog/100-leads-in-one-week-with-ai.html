How I Got 100 Sales Leads in One Week Using AI (Step-by-Step)

Last month, I started a new sales campaign from scratch. No existing pipeline. No warm leads. Just a LinkedIn account and Agentcsx.

7 days later, I had 100 scored, qualified leads in my pipeline. 23 of them were "hot" (scored 80+). 8 had already accepted my connection request. Here's exactly how I did it.

Day 1: Setting Up (15 minutes)

Define the Target

I was selling a B2B SaaS product for marketing teams. My ideal client profile:

  • Title: Head of Marketing, VP Marketing, CMO
  • Company size: 50-500 employees
  • Industry: SaaS, Tech, E-commerce
  • Location: US, UK, Canada

Install and Configure

I installed Agentcsx from the Chrome Web Store. Setup took literally 30 seconds — open LinkedIn, click the extension, done. I entered my business context so the AI understood what I was selling.

Day 2-3: The Hunt (20 minutes/day)

People Search

I ran my first search: "Head of Marketing SaaS." Agentcsx found 127 leads in 4 minutes. Each one was automatically scored by the AI.

Results breakdown:

  • Hot leads (80-100): 18 leads — perfect ICP match
  • Warm leads (50-79): 52 leads — good fit, worth pursuing
  • Cold leads (0-49): 57 leads — less relevant

Content Search (Secret Weapon)

Next, I searched for people who had recently posted about "marketing automation challenges." This found 43 additional leads who were actively discussing the problem I solve.

These content-sourced leads had an average score of 74 — much higher than the general people search average of 58. Makes sense: they're already thinking about my space.

Company Search

Finally, I searched for "SaaS companies 50-200 employees recently funded." Found 15 companies, which yielded 31 more leads when I drilled into their marketing teams.

Day 2-3 total: 201 leads discovered, all scored automatically.

Day 3-4: Prioritize and Clean (10 minutes)

I filtered to hot leads only (80+). That gave me 34 leads. I quickly reviewed each one:

  • Removed 5 that were clearly wrong fits (wrong company type)
  • Removed 2 duplicates
  • Saved the remaining 27 hot leads to my "Q3 Campaign" collection

Then I saved the top 73 warm leads as a separate collection for later.

Day 4-5: Connect (15 minutes/day)

Personalized Connection Requests

I used Agentcsx's AI to generate personalized connection notes for each hot lead. The AI looked at their profile, company, and recent posts to craft unique notes.

Example notes it generated:

"Hi Sarah, I noticed your post about scaling content ops at a lean team — really resonated. I work with marketing leaders on exactly this challenge. Would love to connect and exchange ideas."

I sent 15 connection requests on Day 4 and 12 on Day 5. The built-in safety limits made sure I didn't exceed LinkedIn's thresholds.

Acceptance rate after 48 hours: 8 out of 27 (30%). That's 10x the average cold outreach acceptance rate.

Day 5-7: Engage and Expand

Engage with Acceptors

For the 8 people who accepted, I:

  1. Sent a brief thank-you message (no pitch)
  2. Liked and commented on their recent posts
  3. Waited 3 days before introducing my pitch

Expand to Warm Leads

I repeated the connection process with the next batch of 30 warm leads. Another 15 minutes of work.

Auto-Post for Visibility

I also used Agentcsx's AI posting feature to publish a LinkedIn post about marketing automation challenges. This generated engagement from my target audience and attracted 3 inbound connection requests.

The Results After 7 Days

  • Total leads discovered: 201
  • Leads saved to pipeline: 100
  • Hot leads (80+ score): 27
  • Connection requests sent: 57
  • Connections accepted: 19 (33% rate)
  • Conversations started: 8
  • Total time spent: ~2 hours over 7 days
📈 ROI Breakdown

2 hours of work produced 100 scored leads and 8 active conversations. Using traditional cold outreach at a 2% response rate, I would have needed to send 400+ messages to get the same result — that's 10+ hours of work minimum.

What I'd Do Differently Next Time

  1. Start with content search first. These leads had the highest scores and fastest conversion rates.
  2. Engage before connecting. The leads I commented on before connecting had a 50% acceptance rate vs. 25% for cold connects.
  3. Post more content. My one LinkedIn post generated 3 inbound leads with zero effort. I should have posted daily.

Your Turn

This isn't a complicated system. It's a simple framework:

  1. Install Agentcsx (free, 30 seconds)
  2. Search for your ideal clients across people, content, and companies
  3. Let AI score and prioritize your leads
  4. Send personalized connections to hot leads
  5. Engage, follow up, and close

100 leads in one week. No cold DMs. No spreadsheets. Just AI doing the heavy lifting.
