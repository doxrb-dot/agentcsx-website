Frequently Asked Questions

Is Agentcsx free to use?
Yes! Agentcsx is completely free. No credit card required, no hidden fees, no trial period.
Will this get my LinkedIn account restricted?
Agentcsx has built-in safety limits — 20 connection requests per day and 80 per week. These stay well within LinkedIn's guidelines to keep your account safe.
What data do you collect?
Your leads are stored locally on your device. If you sign in with Google, we optionally sync your campaigns to the cloud so you don't lose them. We never sell or share your data with third parties.
Does it work with LinkedIn Sales Navigator?
Agentcsx works with the standard LinkedIn interface. Sales Navigator is not required, but it's compatible if you have it.
Which browsers are supported?
Agentcsx works on all Chromium-based browsers: Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge, Brave, Opera, and Vivaldi.
How do I uninstall the extension?
Right-click the Agentcsx icon in your browser toolbar → "Remove from Chrome". Your cloud-synced data will remain safe if you ever want to reinstall.
